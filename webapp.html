Interactive Potential Exposure Map

Interactive web map displaying potential exposure to abandoned uranium mines (AUMs) across Navajo Nation. The exposure map is a raster surface with an index value for each cell representing potential exposure. Exposure is predicted through a multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA) methodology developed by Lin et al. (2020) which takes into account meteorological, topographic, soil, and hydrology data.
